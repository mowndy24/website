Meet The Founder

Dr. Carmen Báez-Franceschi

MD, MBA — Founder & Medical Director

Dr. Báez-Franceschi possesses over 20 years of experience as a Board-Certified Pediatrician and maintains medical licenses in Texas, Florida, and Puerto Rico. She has spent over a decade specializing in the treatment of children and adults with neurodevelopmental disorders.

Her innovative approach combines traditional pediatric medicine with integrative therapies, creating the renowned Franceschi Method® — a comprehensive, individualized protocol that has helped thousands of patients across four continents achieve measurable improvement.
